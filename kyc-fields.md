# Comprehensive KYC Client Information Fields

## Personal/Individual Client Information
- Full Legal Name (First, Middle, Last)
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Nationality
- ID Type (National ID, Passport, Driver's License)
- ID Number
- ID Issue Date
- ID Expiry Date
- Tax Identification Number (TIN/KRA PIN)
- Marital Status
- Occupation/Profession
- Employer Name
- Employment Status

## Contact Information
- Primary Phone
- Secondary Phone
- Email Address
- Physical Address (Street, City, County, Postal Code)
- Mailing Address (if different)
- Preferred Contact Method

## Business/Corporate Client Information
- Company Legal Name
- Trading Name (if different)
- Business Registration Number
- Date of Incorporation
- Country of Incorporation
- Business Type/Legal Form (Ltd, LLC, Partnership, Sole Proprietor)
- Industry/Sector
- Number of Employees
- Annual Revenue Range
- Tax ID/KRA PIN
- VAT Registration Number

## Business Contact & Location
- Registered Office Address
- Trading Address (if different)
- Company Phone
- Company Email
- Company Website
- Social Media Links

## Key Personnel (for Corporate Clients)
- Directors/Partners (Name, ID, Position, Shareholding %)
- Authorized Signatories
- Beneficial Owners (>25% ownership)
- Contact Person Name & Position
- Contact Person Phone & Email

## Financial Information
- Bank Name
- Bank Branch
- Account Number
- Account Type
- Payment Terms Preference
- Credit Limit Requested
- Trade References (3)

## Risk Assessment
- Source of Funds/Income
- Purpose of Business Relationship
- Expected Transaction Volume
- Expected Transaction Frequency
- Politically Exposed Person (PEP) Status
- Sanctions List Check

## Document Uploads
- Copy of ID/Passport
- Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Bank Statement)
- Certificate of Incorporation (Corporate)
- CR12 (Certificate of Registration)
- Tax Compliance Certificate
- Bank Statement
- Trade License
- Authorization Letter (for representatives)

## Additional Information
- How did you hear about us?
- Referral Source
- Special Requirements
- Notes/Comments
- Relationship Manager Assigned
- Client Category/Tier
- Client Status (Active, Inactive, Suspended)
- Risk Rating (Low, Medium, High)
- Last KYC Review Date
- Next KYC Review Date

## Compliance & Authorization
- Data Protection Consent
- Terms & Conditions Acceptance
- Marketing Communications Consent
- Client Signature
- Date Signed
- Verified By (Staff Name)
- Verification Date
