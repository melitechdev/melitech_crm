# Document Template Analysis

## Quotation Template (Mayanah Safaris)
- **Header**: Company logo, contact info (Tel, Mail, Web), social media icons
- **Document Info**: Date, Quote No (00206), Ref (Quot-2025/10/012)
- **Client**: TO: MAYANAH SAFARIS
- **Items Table**: NO, ITEM DESCRIPTION, UOM, QTY, UNIT PRICE, Tax 16%, TOTAL
- **Calculations**: SUBTOTAL, 16% VAT, TOTAL (Ksh 81,996.52)
- **Terms of Reference**: 
  1. All prices are in Kenya shillings (KSHs)
  2. VAT is charged where applicable
  3. Quotation is valid for 45days from date of generation
  4. Payment of 75% is expected before commencement of the project
- **Account Details**: Bank, Branch, Acc., Mpesa Paybill, Acc. Number
- **Signature**: For Melitech Solutions
- **Footer**: THANK YOU FOR YOUR BUSINESS! with contact info

## Invoice Template (Jito Glasswork)
- **Header**: Company logo, contact details, P.O BOX
- **Document Info**: Site, Attn, PROFORMA INVOICE, JGWLTD/2022, Date
- **Items Table**: ITEM DESCRIPTION, TOTAL
- **Calculations**: Subtotal, VAT 16%, GRAND TOTAL $
- **Terms of Payment**: 70% on confirmation, 30% on completion
- **Signature**: For and on Behalf of [Company], [Name]
- **Footer**: Thank You For Your Business!

## Receipt Template (CITAM Schools)
- **Header**: Company logo, full address, contact info
- **Document Info**: RECEIPT, DATE, RECEIPT NO. (00175, 00176)
- **Client**: BILL TO: CITAM SCHOOLS - ATHI RIVER
- **Items Table**: ITEM, DESCRIPTION, QTY, UNIT PRICE, TOTAL
- **Calculations**: SUBTOTAL, 16% VAT, TOTAL
- **Signature**: For Melitech Solutions
- **Footer**: THANK YOU FOR YOUR BUSINESS!

## Common Elements
- Auto-increment document numbers
- Company branding (logo, colors)
- Client information
- Itemized table with descriptions, quantities, prices
- Tax calculations (16% VAT)
- Subtotal and Total
- Terms and conditions
- Bank/payment details
- Signature section
- Professional footer
